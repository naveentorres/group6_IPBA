About Us

We are Grade 9 students from Apec schools and our primary goal is to create a fundraising event to raise funds for the residents of Sitio Bebe who are currently undergoing a severe health crisis due to lack of clean watersource, they also have a shortage of health workers, health equipment, medicines and supplies.

Content

One way to promote our fundraising concert is through podcasts. Our group will create our own podcast to promote the benefit concert event and to talk about the topic, report news or stories about the health crisis in Sitio Bebe and what we can do about their situation. We will also invite musicians, students and other guests to talk about the event and share it on various podcast platforms such as Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and Google Podcasts.

We plan to target our content to people who are passionate about the cause and have strong belief in its mission; Fans of the performing artists who are participating in the concert; Philanthropists who actively seek opportunities to contribute to charitable causes and Students and Young Professionals who are passionate about social issues and want to make a difference. These people may attend fundraising concerts as a way to support causes they care about.
